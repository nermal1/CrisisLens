### The Crisis: What Happened and Why

In February 2018, volatility surged unexpectedly, causing products betting against volatility (like XIV) to implode almost overnight.

---

### Timeline of the Crisis

* **Jan 2018:** Markets at all-time highs  
* **Feb 5, 2018:** Volatility spikes sharply  
* **Following days:** Volatility ETFs collapse  

---

### Winners and Losers

<WinnerBox>
- **Volatility Traders:** Profited from spike  
- **Hedged Portfolios:** Protected from downside  
</WinnerBox>

<LoserBox>
- **Inverse Volatility ETFs:** Lost nearly all value  
- **Retail Investors:** Exposed to unexpected risk  
</LoserBox>

---

### Lessons for Investors

* ⚠️ **Complex products carry hidden risks**  
* 📉 **Volatility can spike unexpectedly**  
* 🧠 **Understand what you invest in**
