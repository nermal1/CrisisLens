### The Crisis: What Happened and Why

In response to rising inflation, the Federal Reserve rapidly increased interest rates in 2022. Higher borrowing costs reduced valuations, especially for growth and tech stocks.

---

### Timeline of the Crisis

* **Jan 2022:** Markets begin to decline  
* **March 2022:** First rate hike announced  
* **Mid 2022:** Multiple aggressive hikes  
* **Oct 2022:** Market bottoms  

---

### Winners and Losers

<WinnerBox>
- **Energy Stocks:** Strong performance amid inflation  
- **Value Stocks:** Outperformed growth  
</WinnerBox>

<LoserBox>
- **Tech Stocks:** Hit hardest due to valuation compression  
- **Growth Companies:** Sensitive to interest rates  
</LoserBox>

---

### Lessons for Investors

* 📉 **Interest rates directly affect valuations**  
* ⚠️ **Growth stocks are more vulnerable to rate hikes**  
* 🧠 **Macro policy drives market cycles**
